Tinfoil B.R.C Ratis Bluetooth Elfo
Pecking Order Alfa #2 #3 #4 #5
Description Tinfoil is the alfa of the flock. He is big and has long tail feathers. B.R.C. is the oldest rooster. He is my personal favorite chicken. He has big feet due to bumblefoot. He has pretty long tail feathers. Ratis has one hen who loves him: Fatty Junior. He has the smallest tail feathers. Bluetooth is like a mini version of Tinfoil with shorter tail feathers and slightly different feathers. Elfo is the lowest ranking rooster in the pecking order.
